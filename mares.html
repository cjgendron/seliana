Meet Our Herd

Icelandic Mares
at Seliana

Every horse has a mother — and at Seliana, we believe the mare is the most important part of the breeding equation. Our mares are selected for naturally clear gaits, strong conformation, and easy, pleasant temperaments. We select the best mares to bring your next partner — and your dreams — to life.
