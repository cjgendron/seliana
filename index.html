Our Philosophy

The Icelandic Horse,
at home in America

Everything at Seliana begins with a simple belief: the right horse, in the right hands, can create more joy and delight in a person's life than just about anything else on earth.

The Icelandic horse is extraordinary — and we believe the traits that serve American riders best are worth selecting for deliberately. We prioritize:

· Willing, forgiving, and person-oriented temperament
· Naturally well-separated gaits — the kind that don't depend on a specialist to develop
· Sturdy conformation — solid, capable, and comfortable for anyone to ride

Whether born here or brought here, every horse at Seliana receives the same gift — trust in their humans, built through good hands and genuine care, day after day. They arrive at a new home already settled, already themselves. The result is a horse that feels like it was made for you — because in many ways, it was.

Why Seliana?
